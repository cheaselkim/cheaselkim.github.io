How it Works
Occupancy Data
BACnet
30% HVAC Energy Savings and 1 Year Payback
Komfort IQ works by collecting the most information for each workspace of a commercial office building. KIQ then uses that data for AI algorithms to optimize workspaces for comfort as well as energy savings. The building’s HVAC system is also optimized using the same data and AI algorithms based on current and future demand and current and future weather conditions. This results in improved comfort and proven savings of 30%+ HVAC energy savings with a payback of 1 year.
KIQ works in most large office buildings with VAV HVAC systems and with most BMS systems.

About Us

We are KomfortIQ, where cutting-edge technology meets sustainable solutions. Established in 2016, we are a pioneering HVAC controls company that leverages artificial intelligence to revolutionize the climate control systems of large commercial office buildings. Committed to efficiency and environmental responsibility, our team of experts collaborates at the intersection of technology and real estate, ensuring optimal performance and energy savings. We’re partnering with some of the world's largest real estate companies to demonstrate our commitment to excellence and have garnered a reputation for innovation because we’re not just transforming spaces; we are shaping the future of sustainable and intelligent building management. Join us on this journey towards a greener and more efficient tomorrow.
