Privacy Policy

Thank you for using the products and services of Komfort IQ. Our company values the protection of your privacy, and this Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal data. Please carefully read this document to understand our information processing practices and how you can control the use of your personal data.

1. Information Collection

We may collect various types of information, including but not limited to:

1.1. Personal Information

We may collect personal information such as name, email, phone number, and other information you provide during registration or interaction with our products and services.

1.2. Technical Information

We may automatically collect technical information, such as IP addresses, device types, operating systems, and other information about the use of our products and interaction with them.

2. Use of Information

We use the collected information to improve our products and services and provide you with a more personalized and efficient user experience. We may use your information for:

  • Providing and managing services you requested.
  • Supporting users and responding to your inquiries.
  • Enhancing the functionality and security of our products and services.
  • Analyzing and improving the effectiveness of our marketing efforts.

3. Disclosure of Information

We do not disclose your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as required by law. We may only disclose your information to employees and partners who need it to provide you with Komfort IQ products and services.

4. Information Protection

We prioritize the protection of your information and take necessary security measures to prevent unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure of data.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to your personal information, correct any inaccuracies, or delete your data from our systems.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may periodically update this Privacy Policy, and any changes will be published on our website. We recommend reviewing this document periodically to stay informed about changes.

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the processing of your information, please contact us using the contact information provided on our website.

Thank you for your trust and for using Komfort IQ products and services.
